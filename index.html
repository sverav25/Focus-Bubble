What is Focus Bubble?

Focus Bubble is a unique Pomodoro Timer that helps you stay focused by subtly guiding your attention. It creates a calm, distraction-free space so you can concentrate on your tasks more effectively. Take control of your time and learn with ease.

Why Focus Bubble?

Studies show that most people lose concentration within minutes due to constant distractions, notifications, and tab switching. Focus Bubble was built for this exact problem — to help you resist constant distractions and focus easier.

How to use Focus Bubble?

Pick a task you want to complete, set your preferred timer intervals, and start working. Focus Bubble will gently monitor your attention — if you get distracted, the screen will blur, helping you refocus and stay on track. It's a simple, visual way to keep your mind on what matters most.

Fill up the boxes after every Focus Session and be awarded with Long Break after filling every one of them.
